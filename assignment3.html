    6. Definition

    Funny Drink is a cold beverage mostly consisting of citrus drinks.

    It is typically a sugar-rich drink, and is occasionally caffeinated.

    It is a refreshing drink to be enjoyed year round.

    The exact ingredients or drinks used in Funny Drink change every time it is made.

    There are also no exact measurements or recipe. Just vibes.

    Origins

    Funny Drink was invented by the awesome cool epic engineer, coder, artist, and moon specialist Kai Hughes.

    Kai is very silly and often has multiple open drinks on their desk and in their fridge.

    Thus they decided one fateful day during quarantine to just mix every open drink they had in one cup.

    Funny Drink was born, with the first one likely consisting of lemonade, sprite, and peach juice.

    Historians do not have sufficient evidence to determine the exact combination of drinks in the first Funny Drink,

    and can only speculate what was in it.
